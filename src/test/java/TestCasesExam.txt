TC3 Adding item to the basket -------------------------------
Preconditions:
Login with valid creds

STR:
1. Hover mouse over the 'Макіяж' menu drop-down
2. Click on the 'Мініатюри' menu option
3. Click on any product name
4. Click on the 'Купити' button

ER:
1.'Кошик' text in popup window is displayed
2. Basket counter is increased by one
